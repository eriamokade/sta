WELCOME TO STRONG TOWER ACADEMY

Strong Tower Academy Lagos is a purposely built privately owned educational citadel of learning located at Oke Ota Ona Ikorodu, Lagos.
The school commenced on 26th September 2010 to provide a real-time purpose-driven secondary school with basic qualitative, holistic, and world-class education to complement the good initiatives of the Lagos state government in providing quality education to every child in Lagos and in alignment with the Nigerian Educational policy by extension.

Strong Tower Academy is conceived as a platform for developing productive and purpose-driven leaders whose values are informed by sound Christian virtues, this is an over vision statement at Strong Tower Academy.
The school is duly registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission and approved by the Lagos State Ministry of Education to run secondary and primary schools.

At Strong Tower Academy, our mission is to provide real-time educational solutions in a conducive and serene environment to raise and inspire a generation of visionaries and ensure that stakeholders in child education get the desired returns on their investments.

At STA, our aims and objectives are in line with the standard provided by UNESCO and in total compliance with the regulations of the Lagos state government.

At STA we aim to provide quality education for all and lifelong learning that is enduring through mobilization of science knowledge and policy for sustainable development.

To ensure this is effectively done, we engage students in practical exercises to learn in all subject areas, provide instructional videos for learning, and a personalized online platform that empowers learners to study at their own pace in and outside the classroom.

At STA, we attempt to tailor our learning by addressing the emerging social and ethical challenges that will make our students gain academic excellence and good moral standards.

To ensure this is achieved, our slogan is raising total leaders, without motor, being obedience and diligence. We operate on zero tolerance to exam malpractice!
