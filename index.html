WELCOME TO STRONG TOWER ACADEMY


Strong Tower Academy Lagos is a purposely built privately owned citadel
of learning located at Oke Ota Ona Ikorodu, Lagos since 26th Sept 2010.
It provides purpose-driven secondary school with qualitative, holistic
world-class education complementing Lagos govt initiative and policy.

STA is conceived as a platform for developing productive leaders
with sound Christian virtues; this is the overall vision statement.
The school is registered with CAC and approved by Lagos Ministry
of Education to run both secondary and primary schools officially.

Our mission is to provide real-time education in a serene environment
to inspire visionaries and ensure stakeholders receive full returns.
We aim in line with UNESCO and Lagos state regulations completely.

At STA we provide lifelong learning through science, policy,
and sustainable development principles with student participation.
Practical exercises, instructional videos, and an online platform
empower learners to study at their own pace in and outside class.

We tailor learning to social and ethical challenges for excellence.
Students gain academic success with high moral and Christian values.
Our slogan: raising total leaders with obedience and diligence.
We maintain zero tolerance for exam malpractice at all times.
